ATTACK ON TITAN
9.1/10 2013 - 2023
Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Hajime Isayama. It is widely acclaimed for its intense story, complex characters, and deep philosophical themes.
JUJUTSU KAISEN
8.8/10 2020 - Present
Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. It combines supernatural elements, intense action, and deep emotional storytelling.
DEMON SLAYER
8.7/10 2019 - Present
Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a highly acclaimed Japanese anime series known for its stunning animation, emotional storytelling, and intense battles. It's based on the manga written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge.
ONE PIECE
8.9/10 1999 - Present
One Piece is one of the most iconic and longest-running anime series in history. Created by Eiichiro Oda, it's a high-seas adventure packed with humor, emotion, friendship, and epic battles. The story blends fantasy, pirate lore, and deep world-building like no other anime.
